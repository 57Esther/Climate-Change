CAUSES AND EFFECT OF CLIMATE CHANGE

"Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. These shifts may be natural, but since the 1800s, human activities have been the main driver of climate change, primarily due to the burning of fossil fuels like coal, oil and gas, which produces heat-trapping gases. Burning fossil fuels generates greenhouse gas emissions that act like a blanket wrapped around the Earth, trapping the sun's heat and raising temperatures". "The main greenhouse gases that are causing climate change include carbon dioxide and methane. These come from using gasoline for driving a car or coal for heating a building, for example. Clearing land and cutting down forests can also release carbon dioxide. Agriculture, oil and gas operations are major sources of methane emissions. Energy, industry, transport, buildings, agriculture and land use are among the main sectors causing greenhouse gases."
Source: United Nations

The Effects of climate change are as follows
  1. Hotter Temperature.
  2. More Severe weather
  3. A Warming and Rising Oceans.
  4. Flooding and Risk to Lives.
  5. Increase Drought.
  6. Lack of food.
  7. More Health Risks.
  8. deplacement and Poverty.
  9. Loss of Animals and Species.

Generating Renewable Electricity

Generating Renewable Electricity would not only save you money, it will also contribute to reducing the carbon emmissions of the world electricity network. With millons of people already generating electricity from solar or wind, renewable electricity are quickly becoming more common across the world.
The types of renewable energy are solar panels, wind turbines, hydroelectricity, micro combined heat and power and many more which can be use to power home appliances, electric vehicles and safe our plant from the massive effects of climate change.

Low Carbon Travel

"Shifting from cars to public transportation can reduce up to 2.2 tons of carbon emissions annually per individual".
Source: United Nations
Biking and walking are also great way to reduce the level of carbon emmision on planets earth, making simple life style changes can actually help save our world. simple lifestyel changes can indeed save our planet.

A tree helps prevents erosion, flooding and improve soils. Planting a tree could save one tons of carbon from emissions which causes climate change.

Turning off household devices, like televison, gadgets, ipads, sound systems, wall sockets, kitchen appliances and many more doesn't just help save money on energy bills its also reduce carbon emmision on our planet by thousands of pounds each year.
